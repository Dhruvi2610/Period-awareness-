What Happens During a Period?

Your body is truly amazing! Periods are a healthy and natural process your body goes through to maintain balance.

Click on the steps to learn about your period!

Menstruation

The egg is released from the ovaries during ovulation.

The egg travels through the fallopian tubes towards the uterus.

The uterus lining thickens to prepare for a potential pregnancy.

If no pregnancy occurs, the egg dissolves and is absorbed by the body.

If there is no pregnancy, the uterus sheds its lining, which is called menstruation (period blood).
